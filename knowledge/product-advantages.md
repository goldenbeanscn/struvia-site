# Product Positioning

Struvia exterior systems are designed to improve buildability and simplify exterior construction workflows.

The system should be positioned as:

* fast-install exterior systems
* modular-friendly construction systems
* construction-efficient exterior solutions
* modern architectural exterior systems

Avoid positioning as:

* cheap construction materials
* generic wall panels
* low-cost commodity products
* traditional factory products
