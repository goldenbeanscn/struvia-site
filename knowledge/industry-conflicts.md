# Industry Conflicts

## Traditional Exterior Construction Problems

### Wet Construction

Problems:
- weather dependency
- slow execution
- inconsistent quality
- labor-heavy workflows

---

### Labor Dependency

Problems:
- rising labor costs
- inconsistent installation
- difficult scaling
- coordination pressure

---

### Fragmented Facade Execution

Problems:
- too many onsite variables
- facade inconsistency
- sequencing complexity
- difficult repeatability

---

### Exterior Complexity

Problems:
- installation overload
- workflow inefficiency
- coordination burden
- unpredictable project delivery

---

# Struvia Position

Struvia represents:

- installation-first systems

- repeatable installation logic
- reduced construction complexity
