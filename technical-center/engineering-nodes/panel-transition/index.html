Inspection System

Inspection should focus on the physical control point, not only the finished appearance.

Joint line

Check straightness and gap consistency.

Thermal break

Look for open gaps or damaged insulation at transition.

Fixing stability

Confirm fastener hold without surface distortion.

Trim relationship

Connector trim should not block drainage or create a proud edge.

Failure Cause

Panel transition failures are cumulative: small alignment or fixing errors become visible and functional problems across the wall field.

Failure Analysis

Misaligned Rhythm

Panel transition accumulates visual or dimensional error.

Thermal Gap

Transition gap interrupts insulation continuity.

Weak Fixing Zone

Screw line misses substrate or creates uneven restraint.
