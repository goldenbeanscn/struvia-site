Cross-System Relationship Map

Exterior system decisions are connected. A trim decision affects waterproofing, installation sequence, thermal continuity, wind behavior, and site inspection.

Waterproof <-> Accessories

Overlap, drainage, corners, openings, and lower terminations depend on trim sequencing.

Installation <-> Thermal

Panel seating, cut quality, and joint closure affect insulation continuity.

Performance <-> Product Structure

Wind, thermal, moisture, and fire behavior require layer and assembly context.

Logistics <-> Site Protection

Loading, unloading, storage, and handling determine whether panels remain installable.
