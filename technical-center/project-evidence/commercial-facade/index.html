Project Evidence / Commercial Facade

Commercial facade execution validation.

Public-facing facade evidence for alignment, opening treatment, corner closure, waterproof sequencing, trim continuity, and inspection records.

Application Logic

High visibility, opening density, corner quality, facade rhythm, and evidence-backed inspection.

Engineering Project Logic

Commercial facades require project evidence that visual alignment and engineering control points work together: openings, corners, trim closure, drainage, and field joints must be checked rather than treated as finish-only details.

Selection Reason

Exterior panel systems provide repeatable facade fields, trim-managed transitions, and practical inspection points for visible wall areas.

Waterproof Strategy

Opening flashing, trim closure, drainage paths, and overlap direction must be documented with close-up evidence.

Buildability

Repeatable sequencing helps keep alignment, corners, and openings under control across public-facing elevations.

Risk Prevention & Inspection

Before Install

Confirm facade layout, opening sequence, corner trim allowance, and visible panel rhythm.

During Install

Inspect panel seating, trim continuity, flashing closure, and screw-zone restraint.

After Install

Record wide facade context plus close-ups for openings, corners, joints, and base drainage.

Failure Prevention

Primary risks are wrong overlap, open cut edges, blocked drainage, corner misalignment, and missing close-up evidence.
