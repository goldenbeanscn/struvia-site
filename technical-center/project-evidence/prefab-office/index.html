Project Evidence / Prefab Office

Prefab office execution validation.

Modular office evidence for opening treatment, panel sequence, trim closure, thermal continuity, and transport-aware site installation.

Application Logic

Fast assembly, repeated modules, frequent openings, controlled trim details, and site reassembly checks.

Engineering Project Logic

A prefab office depends on repeatable wall modules, predictable installation, protected openings, and evidence that transport or site handling did not compromise edge and trim details.

Selection Reason

Lightweight exterior panels support modular-friendly sequencing and reduce onsite complexity.

Waterproof Strategy

Opening flashing, jamb closure, sill release, and trim continuity must be checked at every office opening.

Buildability

Repeatable panels and trims reduce site improvisation when modules arrive for assembly.

Risk Prevention & Inspection

Before Install

Check transported module condition, edge protection, opening dimensions, and flashing sequence.

During Install

Inspect interlock seating, screw fixing, trim closure, and sill release.

After Install

Record opening close-ups, module joints, trim continuity, and final facade context.

Failure Prevention

Primary risks are opening leakage, trim conflict, thermal discontinuity, transport damage, and forced alignment.
