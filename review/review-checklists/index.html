Review Checklists

Reusable engineering review checklists.

Checklist system for evaluating engineering documentation quality by page type, not by surface-level content volume.

Checklist Rule

A page passes when its execution logic, risk control, evidence path, and system relationships are auditable.

Technical Subpages

Execution references for field conditions such as window flashing, base drainage, interlock sequence, outside corner, and thermal continuity joints.

Execution Logic

Includes overview, control layer, hold point, and assembly sequence.

Risk Architecture

Explains what fails, why it fails, and how sequencing causes the failure.

Inspection Workflow

Includes before-closure and after-closure checkpoints.

Evidence Slots

Defines exact close-ups, wide frames, sequence frames, and risk frames needed.

Node Pages

Detail logic pages for inside corners, outside corners, starter trim, edge closure, window flashing, drainage exits, overlaps, and panel transitions.

Node Definition

Identifies the physical junction and control layers involved.

Layer Relationship

Shows panel skin, core, backing, trim, substrate, fastener, outlet, or overlap relation.

Failure Mode

Connects physical geometry to moisture, deformation, corrosion, or thermal risk.

Evidence Protocol

Specifies close detail, workflow frame, inspection image, and failure example.

Waterproof Pages

Water Path

Defines how water moves down, out, and away from cut edges or openings.

Drainage Logic

Identifies outlets, release gaps, drainage exits, and blocked-path risks.

Lap Direction

Checks reverse-lap risk at head, base, corner, trim, and overlap conditions.

Moisture Evidence

Requires annotated water-path diagrams and real drainage/closure evidence.

Installation Pages

Sequencing

Shows order of cleaning, alignment, seating, fixing, inspection, and repetition.

Installer Risk

Explains dragging, twisting, premature fixing, over-fixing, edge damage, and accumulated error.

Fastener Logic

Links screw zone, substrate, restraint, and distortion risk.

Field Evidence

Requires before, during, after, and final joint images.

Thermal Pages

Continuity

Distinguishes panel-field insulation from whole-assembly thermal behavior.

Bridge Review

Checks fasteners, trims, openings, substrate paths, and cut edges.

Condensation Risk

Explains air movement, cold surface risk, and moisture interaction without unsupported claims.

Thermal Evidence

Requires joint close-ups, cut-edge core records, and transition evidence.

Logistics Pages

Handling Logic

Explains distributed lifting, deformation prevention, edge protection, and safe workflow.

Storage Method

Checks dry storage, pallet spacing, gravity deformation, covering, and ventilation.

Loading Sequence

Connects pallet support, container fit, impact prevention, and unloading risk.

Logistics Evidence

Requires loading frames, pallet photos, protection close-ups, and handling visuals.
